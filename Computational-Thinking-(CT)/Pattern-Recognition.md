# [Pattern Recognition]()

**Pattern Recognition** is the ability to look for and analyze similarities and differences within problems or across different problems.It is a skill that helps identify recurring structures, behaviors, or characteristics among objects or concepts. After a problem has been broken down through [**decomposition**](), pattern recognition allows us to observe relationships between its parts.
This skill is essential in problem-solving because it helps identify solutions that can be reused for similar problems, rather than starting from scratch every time.
By recognizing patterns, we can avoid unnecessary duplication and work more efficiently.

---

# Why It Matters

* It helps reuse existing solutions for similar problems
* It reduces repetitive work and duplication
* It improves efficiency in problem-solving
* It makes complex problems easier to understand by revealing structure
